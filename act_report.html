Insights and Visualizations¶

After completing the entire wrangling process, we move on to analysis and visualization.

I have put before myself 3 questions that interest me:

  • What are the most common names?

  • Which breed gets the highest rating on average?

  • What is the most common stage of the dog in a DataFrame?

To answer these questions I had to pull the data I needed from the recently cleared data. I decided to use the SQLite program. First, I converted the cleaned data to a CSV file and downloaded it to my PC. Then I loaded this file into the program and, using SQL queries, I received the DataFrames I needed. I transferred these data frames back to a csv file and download to Jupyter Notebook. Using this data and matplotlib I created visualizations to answer my questions.

What are the most common names?

As we can see from the visualization, the most frequent name of the dog is Charlie(11). Then follows Tucker, Penny, Oliver, Lucy(10). Winston, Sadie, Lola and Bo appeared 9 times.

Which breed gets the highest rating on average?

As we can see from the visualization, the top rated breeds are Saluki and Pomeranian (~1.25).

What is the most common stage of the dog in a DataFrame?

As we can see from the pie chart, the most(more than a half) common stage of a dog is pupper(66.37%). Then follows doggo and puppo with 24.02% and 7.21% respectively. The smallest share belongs to floofer(2.40%)
